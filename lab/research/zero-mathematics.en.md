## 1. From "Empty Position" to "Existence": The Identity Revolution of "0" and the Misunderstanding of "Non-Existence"

Reviewing history's long river reveals a surprising fact: "0" was not an obvious concept. Through extensive epochs, this star defining "nothing" was absent from the sky of human civilization.
