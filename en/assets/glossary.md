## Core Concepts

### Meta-Void

> **Definition:** Full-spectrum structural field not yet activated, silent superposition state of infinite possibilities
> 
> **Axioms:**
> - `Axiom 1.0`: H(MetaVoid) = ∞, T = 0 when θ = ∅
> - `Axiom 1.2`: MetaVoid ≠ ∅ (Non-existence ≠ Void)
>
> **Module:** [`core/meta-void/tension-structure`](../core/meta-void/tension-structure.md)

---

### Snapshot (Five-Dimensional Snapshot)

> **Definition:** Spatiotemporal quantized unit of reality, discrete sampling and rendering result of consciousness upon Meta-Void
>
> **Characteristics:**
> - Discreteness: Non-continuous frame sequence
> - Locality: Rendering output of single θ path
> - Instantaneity: One frame of "now"
>
> **Module:** [`engine/snapshot-service/discrete-generation`](../engine/snapshot-service/discrete-generation.md)

---

### Consciousness-Dimension

> **Definition:** In MVM framework, axiomatized as universe's projection dimension (not subsidiary attribute of matter)
>
> **Sub-parameters:**
> - **ω (Spectrum)**: Determines resolution → [`spectrum-omega`](../core/consciousness/spectrum-omega.md)
> - **θ (Path)**: Determines access position → [`path-theta`](../core/consciousness/path-theta.md)
>
> **Axiom:**
> - `Axiom 2.2`: ∂ω/∂θ = 0 (ω and θ are orthogonally independent)

---

### Mapping

> **Definition:** Process through which reality is indexed via "consciousness path × potentiality structure"
>
> **Core Formula:**
> ```
> Reality(Snapshot) = f(MetaVoid ⊗ ConsciousnessPath_{ω,θ})
> ```
>
> **Module:** [`engine/mapping-logic/formula-S`](../engine/mapping-logic/formula-S.md)

---

### Potentiality-Field

> **Definition:** Structural distribution of potentiality within Meta-Void, possessing non-uniform density characteristics
>
> **Attributes:**
> - Density ρ: Degree of potentiality concentration
> - Topology τ: Accessible path structure
>
> **Module:** [`core/meta-void/potentiality-field`](../core/meta-void/potentiality-field.md)

---

### Tension

> **Definition:** Manifestation intensity when Meta-Void is activated by consciousness path
>
> **Axioms:**
> - `Axiom 1.1`: T > 0 when θ ≠ ∅
> - T ∝ ω (Tension positively correlates with spectrum)
>
> **Module:** [`core/meta-void/tension-structure`](../core/meta-void/tension-structure.md)

---

### Snapshot-Chain

> **Definition:** Ordered linkage of multiple snapshots, forming foundation of continuous experience
>
> **Phenomena Explained:**
> - Memory: Path backtracing to prior snapshots
> - Premonition: Foreshock perception of high-density regions
> - Epiphany: Cross-chain transition
>
> **Module:** [`engine/snapshot-service/snapshot-chains`](../engine/snapshot-service/snapshot-chains.md)

---

### Observation (O)

> **Definition:** One of three consciousness parameter elements, trigger executing final confirmation and state lock
>
> **Functions:**
> - Select and lock specific result from foreshock state
> - Complete "collapse" from superposition to determinate state
> - Analogous to measurement in quantum mechanics
>
> **Module:** [`engine/mapping-logic/formula-S`](../engine/mapping-logic/formula-S.md)

---

### Foreshock

> **Definition:** Process of structural perturbation accumulation and incubation before snapshot generation
>
> **Characteristics:**
> - Continuous accumulation of manifestation tension
> - Exhibits non-linear threshold effects
> - May correspond to consciousness phenomena like premonition, inspiration
>
> **Module:** [`core/meta-void/potentiality-field`](../core/meta-void/potentiality-field.md)

---

### Manifestation-Tension

> **Definition:** Structural perturbation echo produced when Non-existence tension structure is touched by consciousness
>
> **Characteristics:**
> - Precursor not of "thing," but of potentiality response
> - Possesses directionality but not substantiality
> - Constitutes ontological basis for phenomena like premonition, inspiration
>
> **Module:** [`core/meta-void/tension-structure`](../core/meta-void/tension-structure.md)

---

### Structural-Density

> **Definition:** Distribution of potentiality richness and activatability across different regions in Non-existence tension field
>
> **Attributes:**
> - ρₛ: Structural complexity indicator
> - Affects ease of manifestation
> - High-density regions suitable for complex manifestation
>
> **Formula:** `ρₛ(region) ∝ complexity(S_possible)`
>
> **Module:** [`core/meta-void/tension-structure`](../core/meta-void/tension-structure.md)

---

### Manifestation-Capacity

> **Definition:** System's capacity to continuously generate reality snapshots, MVM's new definition of "vitality"
>
> **Assessment Dimensions:**
> - Breadth and height of ω
> - Density and complexity of θ
> - Tension sensing and response capability
>
> **Module:** [`modules/life-definition`](../modules/life-definition.md)
